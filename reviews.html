Devil You Know

'Anyone who likes Georgette Heyer and is content to a listen to a romance which leaves the most sensual scenes in the reader's imagination is likely to deal very well with the Ledburys. The Devil You Know was a thoroughly gratifying tale of love, friendship, and redemption, with which I was sorry to part at he end and I know I will return to again.'
